Sunsets By The Waters

Witness the brilliance of the sunset as it ushers in the night along sandy beaches on the shores of spraying ocean waters

Behind The Lens

Gloria Joy is a photographer passionate about capturing the true beauty of sunsets by the beach. Through Sunsets By The Waters, she strives to capture the ever-changing colours, peaceful atmosphere, and natural elegance of the coastline at golden hour. Each photograph is a celebration of nature's artistry, preserving moments of calm and wonder that inspire connection and reflection.
