Sunsets By The Waters

Witness the brilliance of the sunset as it ushers in the night along sandy beaches on the shores of spraying ocean waters. This platform exists for dreamers, interior designers, and coastal lovers who want to bring the tranquil energy of the sea into their everyday spaces. Every image featured on the site is captured during Golden hour,that brief window right before dusk when light turns to warm amber, rose gold, and soft violet.

Behind The Lens

Gloria Joy is a photographer passionate about capturing the true beauty of sunsets by the beach. Through Sunsets By The Waters, she strives to capture the ever-changing colours, peaceful atmosphere, and natural elegance of the coastline at golden hour. Each photograph is a celebration of nature's artistry, preserving moments of calm and wonder that inspire connection and reflection.
